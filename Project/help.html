Help & Guidelines

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

1. How do I log in to the admin dashboard?

To log in, enter your username and password in the login form. If you do not have an account, please sign up first.

2. How do I manage user reviews?

Navigate to the "User Reviews" section from the sidebar to view, edit, or delete user reviews.

3. What analytics can I view?

In the "Site Analytics" section, you can view total visits, unique users, page views, and more.

General Tips
